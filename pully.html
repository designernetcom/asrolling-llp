Pulley

At AS Rolling Shutter LLP, we manufacture and supply high-quality pulleys designed for rolling shutters, ensuring smooth and efficient operation. Our pulleys are crafted from premium materials to provide durability, high load-bearing capacity, and long-term reliability.

A Pulley is a crucial component of a rolling shutter system, helping in the smooth lifting and lowering of the shutter curtain. It works by guiding the shutter’s movement while reducing friction, making manual and automatic operation effortless.

Key Benefits

  • Smooth Operation – Ensures effortless rolling shutter movement with minimal resistance.
  • High Durability – Made from strong and rust-resistant materials for long-lasting performance.
  • Load-Bearing Strength – Capable of handling heavy shutters without deformation.
