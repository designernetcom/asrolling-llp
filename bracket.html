Bracket

At AS Rolling Shutter LLP, we manufacture and supply high-quality brackets designed for rolling shutters and other structural applications. Our brackets are built with precision engineering, ensuring strength, durability, and reliability for long-term use.

Rolling shutter brackets are essential components that provide stable support for the shutter mechanism. Made from high-grade materials, our brackets are designed to handle heavy loads and ensure smooth shutter operation.

Key Features

  • Heavy-duty construction for maximum durability.
  • Rust-resistant coating for extended lifespan.
  • Precision-cut for accurate fitting and easy installation.
  • Compatible with manual and automatic rolling shutters.

Product Specifications :

Material Mild Steel / Stainless Steel
Thickness 3mm, 5mm, 8mm, and 10mm
Load Capacity 50 kg to 500 kg
Types of Brackets Standard Brackets / Adjustable Brackets / Custom Brackets
Finish Powder-coated, Zinc-plated
